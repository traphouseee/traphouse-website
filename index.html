About Us

TRAPHOUSE is a dynamic rap group featuring the talents of Dominic, Daniel, Asher, Colten, Michael, Logan, and Leo. Known for their innovative style and captivating performances, TRAPHOUSE has made a significant impact on the rap scene. Our music embodies the spirit of the streets and the energy of the trap life.

Featuring appearances by the legendary Big Jefferson and the dynamic BIGMONET, TRAPHOUSE continues to push the boundaries of rap music and culture.
